Genshin Impact :

The World of Teyvat and the Traveler Who Lost Their Twin

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play open-world action RPG. Players take on the role of a traveler who has been separated from their twin in a world called Teyvat.

Alongside their emergency food companion, Paimon, you'll explore a vast and vibrant world filled with challenges, puzzles, and secrets to uncover.

Exciting Combat System: Combine Elements to Create Dazzling Combos

The core gameplay revolves around building a party of up to four characters, each with unique elemental abilities.

Use of these elements in combat creates exciting combos and allows players to tackle diverse enemies.

Teyvat: Land of 7 Nations Waiting to be Discovered

Teyvat is divided into seven distinct nations, each with its own culture, story, and landscape. Players can freely explore these regions, discovering hidden treasures, completing quests for quirky characters, and delving into deep dungeons.

World of Genshin Impact is constantly expanding with new regions, characters, and storylines added through regular updates, keeping players engaged and eager to see what's next.
