Keqing

As the Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing, Keqing is someone who seeks her own answers instead of idly letting chaos run amok in Liyue. She chooses her own path with her own power and ability, instead of letting the gods determine her fate.

She's known for her efficiency, work ethic, and dedication to her duties. Despite her serious demeanor, Keqing also possesses a rebellious streak and questions the traditional reliance on gods.
